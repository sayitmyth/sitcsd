The Department Of Computer Science


Step into the realm of Computer Science at Shetty Institute of Technology, Kalburgi – a vibrant epicenter of discovery, creativity, and technological progression. Our unwavering commitment is to sculpt the visionaries of tomorrow's digital frontier, ushering in a new era of computer science professionals poised to revolutionize the digital landscape.



Vision

Our vision is rooted in reality, driven by the practical needs of our students and the demands of the ever-evolving technology landscape. We are dedicated to providing a quality education that equips our students for success in their careers



Mission

At the Computer Science Department of Shetty Institute of Technology, Kalburgi, our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that empowers our students to become proficient, adaptable, and ethically responsible professionals in the field of computer science.

  • High-Quality Education
  • Hands-On Learning
  • Innovation and Research
  • Diversity and Inclusion
  • Ethical and Social Responsibility
